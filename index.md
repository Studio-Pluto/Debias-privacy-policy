This Privacy Policy explains how the **Debias** mobile application ("Debias", "the app", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use it. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Debias is an educational app for learning about cognitive biases and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use it.

By using Debias, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Summary (the short version)

- **We do not collect or store your personal information on any server.** Debias has no backend, no user accounts, and no login.
- **Your activity stays on your device.** Quiz progress, diagnostic results and history, and your settings are saved only in local storage on your phone.
- **Third-party services may collect data.** We show ads through Google AdMob and offer an optional purchase through Google Play Billing. These Google services collect data according to their own privacy policies.
- **You can remove ads.** A one-time "Remove Ads" in-app purchase disables advertising.

---

## 2. Information We Handle

### a) Information stored only on your device
Debias saves the following locally on your device so the app can function. This information is **not transmitted to us** and is not accessible to us:

- Quiz answers, scores, and mastery/progress tracking
- Bias self-assessment ("diagnostic") answers, results, and history
- App preferences and settings (such as theme or language)
- Whether you have purchased "Remove Ads"

This data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear the app's data.

### b) Information collected automatically by third-party services
We use third-party services that may automatically collect certain information when you use the app. We do not control this collection. Depending on the service, this may include:

- Device identifiers (including the Android Advertising ID)
- Approximate, non-precise location inferred from your IP address
- Device and connection information (device model, operating system version, network type)
- Ad interaction data (ads shown, clicks)
- Crash and diagnostic information generated by the underlying Google libraries

See **Section 4 (Third-Party Services)** for the specific providers and links to their privacy policies.

### c) Purchases
If you buy the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by **Google Play Billing**. We do **not** receive or store your payment card details, billing address, or other payment information. Google provides us only with confirmation of whether a purchase was completed.

## 3. How We Use Information

Because Debias does not collect personal information on our own servers, we use information only to:

- Process and restore your "Remove Ads" purchase through Google Play

## 5. Advertising and Your Choices

Debias displays ads (including interstitial ads) through Google AdMob unless you purchase "Remove Ads."

- **Personalized ads:** Where required by law (for example, in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland), Google's consent mechanism may ask for your consent before showing personalized ads. You can change your choice as permitted by that mechanism.
- **Reset or limit your Advertising ID:** You can reset or delete your Android Advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings under **Settings → Google → Ads**.
- **Remove ads entirely:** Purchase the one-time "Remove Ads" option in the app.

## 6. App Permissions

Debias requests the following Android permissions:

- **Internet (network access)** — required to load ads and content images, and to process purchases.
- **Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)** — used only to deliver optional reminders/notifications from the app. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
- **Run at startup (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED)** — used to re-schedule reminders after the device restarts.

Debias does **not** request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, precise GPS location, photos, or files.
